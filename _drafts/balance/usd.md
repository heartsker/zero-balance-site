# Stuck With Cents on Your USD Apple Account? Here's How to Clear $0.01-$0.99

_Updated May 2026. For US App Store accounts billed in USD._

The annoying part about US App Store leftovers is the price floor. Apple's smallest standard app and IAP price is **$0.99**. If you have $0.43, $0.67, or $0.09 sitting on your account, there's no single item on the US store you can buy to match. You can't refund it, you can't transfer it, and Apple's region-change blocker treats $0.01 the same as $100.

Here is exactly how to clear small USD remainders.

## Why tiny USD leftovers happen

Five usual culprits:

1. **Half-canceled subscriptions.** You canceled Apple Music or News+ mid-period; Apple refunded the unused days as store credit. The pro-rated amount often lands at something like $4.43 - and then you spent the $4 chunk, leaving $0.43.
2. **Gift card overspend.** You redeemed a $25 gift card, bought a $24.31 subscription, left $0.69 stranded.
3. **Refunded purchase.** You requested a refund through [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com); Apple returned the money as store credit rather than to your card. Now you have $2.99 (or whatever the original price was) sitting around.
4. **Pre-paid subscription change.** You switched from a monthly to an annual subscription; Apple credited the unused monthly portion. That credit is rarely a round dollar.
5. **Promo or apology credit.** Apple support occasionally drops $0.05-$0.50 as goodwill. Stays stuck forever otherwise.

## The four real options for USD leftovers

### Option 1: Buy something for more than your balance (with card overshoot)

Find any item priced above your leftover. A $0.99 app, a $1.29 song, a $1.99 IAP. Buy it. Apple uses your balance first and charges your card the rest.

**Example**: balance $0.43 → buy $0.99 app → balance hits $0.00, card charged $0.56.

**Requires**: a valid US payment method (US-issued card, US Apple Pay, US PayPal) on your account.

**Doesn't work for you if**: you've removed your payment method (common when leaving the US storefront).

### Option 2: Ask Apple Support to zero it out

Apple Support clears small USD balances on request. They keep the money.

**How**: [getsupport.apple.com](https://getsupport.apple.com) → search "Apple Account balance" → "Clear Apple ID balance" → chat or callback. Tell them the amount and your reason (changing country is the strongest reason).

**Success rate**: very high under $1, lower above $5.

**Time**: 15-45 minutes including hold time.

### Option 3: Use Zero Balance to auto-match the amount

_Disclosure: I built [Zero Balance](https://apps.apple.com/app/apple-store/id6761912988?pt=128302223&ct=zerobalance-pro-site&mt=8), and Option 3 is how it works._

Zero Balance combines small consumable IAPs across 8 price tiers to add up exactly to your leftover. You type the amount, the app shows the plan, you confirm each purchase. 60-90 seconds end to end. No access to your Apple ID, no tracking.

**Why it can match amounts under $0.99 that no single app can match**: because it can stack multiple small consumables. A $0.78 leftover might be cleared as $0.49 + $0.29; a $0.09 leftover gets matched to the closest tier and the small overage goes to the next confirmation.

**Requires**: just an iOS device. No payment method needed if your balance covers the IAPs.

### Option 4: Top up to a round number, then spend in one go

If you have $0.43 and you want exactly one purchase, redeem a $0.56 worth of gift card credit to get to $0.99, then buy any $0.99 app. Net change: you've added $0.56 of your own money to escape the loop.

This is the least sensible option for most people but worth mentioning because some forums recommend it.

## What does NOT work for USD leftovers

- **Family Sharing.** Balance is per-Apple-ID. Family members cannot spend your balance. [Full debunk →](/en/family-sharing-apple-balance-myth/)
- **Apple Pay transfer.** Apple Account balance cannot be moved to Apple Cash, Apple Pay, or a bank.
- **Buying physical items at Apple Store.** Apple Account balance only covers digital purchases (apps, media, subscriptions, iCloud). It cannot pay for a physical iPhone or accessory at apple.com or in-store.
- **"Converter" services on Telegram / Discord.** These are scams. They will ask for your Apple ID password and either steal it or charge you and disappear.

## Common USD-specific scenarios

**$0.99 stuck and no card on file (you're moving abroad)**
Apple Support reset is the cleanest path. Mention "changing country" and you'll almost always get a yes.

**$2.99-$4.99 from a refunded app, want to use it**
Buy an app or in-app purchase you actually want at exactly that price - the US App Store has many items priced at those tiers.

**$0.07 from a pro-rated subscription**
Apple Support will almost certainly absorb this on request. It's not worth automating; one chat session ends it.

**A round-dollar balance ($5.00, $10.00, $25.00) and you want it gone**
You don't have a leftover problem - you have a "use my credit" problem. Buy an app, song, or subscription you actually want at that exact price.

## FAQ

**Will Apple ever introduce items priced below $0.99 in the US App Store?**
Apple sets the minimum tier and has held $0.99 for over a decade. There's no public roadmap to lower it.

**Why is the US store more restrictive on small purchases than some other regions?**
Pricing tiers are set by Apple per storefront based on tax and payment-processing economics. The US tier minimum is among the highest in Apple's price band.

**My balance shows $0.00 but region change is still blocked. Why?**
Other blockers exist: active subscription, pending refund, pre-order, family-organizer status. See [full change-country guide →](/en/change-apple-id-country-with-balance/).

**Do these methods also work for GBP, EUR, or other currencies?**
Yes, with different price floors. GBP minimum is £0.99, EUR is €0.49-€0.99 depending on country, INR is ₹15. Currency-specific guides are coming - in the meantime, the [main remove-leftover guide](/en/remove-leftover-apple-id-balance/) covers any currency.
